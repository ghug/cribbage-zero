About

An open-source, from-scratch AlphaZero-style cribbage trainer. This device plays cribbage against itself and learns from it — a neural network guided by Monte-Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) — then saves the trained network to GitHub.

Two modes (toggle on the main screen): this on-device trainer, or a Cloudflare worker that contributes self-play to a shared bus. The net is force-pushed to the repo's net branch.
